Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

PilotTime ("the App") is a flight hour logging utility for pilots. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

PilotTime does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter (flight time entries and labels) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and is never sent anywhere.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

The App optionally uses your device's microphone to allow voice-based time entry. Speech processing is performed on-device by the operating system's built-in speech recognition engine. No audio recordings are stored or transmitted by the App.

Third-Party Services

PilotTime does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Retention

All flight log data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear" button within the App. Uninstalling the App also removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
